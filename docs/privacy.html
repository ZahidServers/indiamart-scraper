Privacy Policy

IndiaMart Lead Manager Scraper — by Webaon

Effective Date: June 2026  ·  Contact: info@webaon.com

Summary: This extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. Everything stays in your browser.

1. Data We Collect

This extension does not collect any personal data about you or track your usage in any way.

The extension reads lead information that is already visible in your browser on the IndiaMart Lead Manager page — such as Buyer Name, Mobile, Product, City, and Message. This data is stored temporarily in your browser's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local) so you can export it. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never sent to any server (including Webaon's servers)
  • Is not shared with any third party
  • Is only accessible to this extension within your browser

2. Local Storage

Lead data collected during a scraping session is stored in Chrome's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local) on your device only. This storage is:

  • Local to your browser profile on your machine
  • Cleared immediately when you click Clear & Reset in the extension
  • Not synced across devices — Chrome Sync is not used
  • Not readable by any website or other extension

3. Permissions Explained

Each permission this extension requests is used solely to make the scraper function.

activeTab To read lead data from the IndiaMart page currently open in your browser. Only activates when you click the extension icon.
scripting To inject the scraper script into the IndiaMart tab so it can interact with the lead grid. Only runs on seller.indiamart.com.
storage To save collected leads locally in your browser so they survive popup opens and closes during a scraping session.

The host permission https://seller.indiamart.com/* restricts all extension activity exclusively to IndiaMart's seller portal. The extension cannot run on any other website.

4. Network Requests

This extension makes no network requests to any external server operated by Webaon or any third party.

The only outbound connection made by the extension's UI is loading the Inter font from Google Fonts (a standard browser request). This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No lead data or user data is included in this request.

5. Third-Party Libraries

This extension bundles SheetJS (xlsx.min.js) for Excel file generation. This library runs entirely within your browser and makes no network requests. It is licensed under the Apache License 2.0.

6. Children's Privacy

This extension is designed for use by IndiaMart sellers and business professionals. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be included in the next release of the extension. Continued use of the extension after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
