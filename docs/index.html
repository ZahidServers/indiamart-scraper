IndiaMart Lead Manager Scraper

Automatically collect all your IndiaMart leads and export them to Excel or CSV — free, fast, and fully local.

Chrome Extension SPA-aware Pause & Resume Excel + CSV Export No data leaves your browser Manifest V3

Auto-pagination

Clicks Next automatically through every page of your Lead Manager without any manual work.

Excel & CSV Export

Download leads instantly as .xlsx (with column widths auto-sized) or .csv with UTF-8 BOM support.

Pause & Resume

Stop mid-scrape and pick up where you left off. All collected leads are preserved in storage.

100% Private

All data stays in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server, ever.

SPA-aware

Detects IndiaMart's React-based page updates without any reloads. Works with the live DOM.

Auto-resume

If the page unexpectedly refreshes, the scraper automatically resumes from where it left off.

Installation

1

Download the extension

Download or clone this repository to your computer.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

In Google Chrome, navigate to chrome://extensions

3

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle Developer mode in the top-right corner of the Extensions page.

4

Load unpacked

Click Load unpacked and select the indiamart-scraper folder.

5

Pin the extension

Click the puzzle icon in Chrome's toolbar and pin IndiaMart Lead Scraper for easy access.

How to Use

Make sure you are on seller.indiamart.com/messagecentre in List View before clicking Start.
1

Log in to IndiaMart

Go to your seller dashboard and open Lead Manager.

2

Switch to List View

Click the List View icon so your leads appear in a grid/table layout.

3

Click Start

Open the extension popup and click Start. The scraper will automatically page through all your leads.

4

Export your data

Once complete — or at any point — click CSV or Excel to download your leads.

Permissions

This extension requests only the minimum permissions needed to operate.

activeTab Read the lead data from the IndiaMart tab currently open in your browser.
scripting Inject the scraper script into the IndiaMart page to interact with the lead grid.
storage Save collected leads in your browser so they persist while the popup opens and closes.
The extension is restricted to seller.indiamart.com only and makes no external network requests.
